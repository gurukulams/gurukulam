The Three Pillars

  1. Focused Learning
  2. Guided Learning
  3. Personalized Learning

Focused Learning

  1. Concentrated learning on specific subjects or skills
  2. Enhancing Depth of Knowledge
  3. Real-world Examples

Guided Learning

  1. Guidance and mentorship in the learning process
  2. Providing Expertise and Support
  3. Case Studies

Personalized Learning

  1. Tailoring education to individual needs
  2. Fostering Self-Motivation and Engagement
  3. Adaptive Learning Platforms

Benefits

  1. Improved Academic Performance
  2. Enhanced Critical Thinking and Problem-Solving Skills
  3. Increased Motivation and Engagement
  4. Better Preparation for the Future Job Market

Challenges

  1. Cost (Technology, Infrastructure and Profit)
  2. New & Untested Methods
  3. Disconnected Solutions
  4. Resistance to Change

Responsible System

  1. Built for by Teachers/Students
  2. Affordable and Approchable
  3. Build Confidentce vs Fear
  4. Encouage Interest vs Competition
