--- title: 'Refund Policy for Gurukulams Online Learning Platform' weight: 2 ---

1. Refund Eligibility:

  • Refunds are applicable for courses purchased directly through Gurukulams.
  • Refund requests must be made within 7 days of the course purchase date.

2. Valid Refund Reason:

  • Refunds are considered for technical issues preventing course access.

3. Refund Process:

  • Raise Support Ticket within the refund period.
  • Clearly state the technical issue.
  • Approved refunds will be credited for future course purchases.

4. Non-eligible Circumstances:

  • Refunds not granted for completed courses or change of mind.

We appreciate your commitment to learning with Gurukulams.
