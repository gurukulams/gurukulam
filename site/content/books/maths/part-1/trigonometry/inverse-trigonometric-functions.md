\\( \tan(\alpha + \beta)=\dfrac{\tan \alpha + \tan \beta}{1-\tan \alpha \tan \beta};\tan(\alpha - \beta)=\dfrac{\tan \alpha - \tan \beta}{1+\tan \alpha \tan \beta}\\)
