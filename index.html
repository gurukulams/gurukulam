How IT Works

  • Picking a Candidate

    We identify potential candidates through trusted sources ( i.e. Colleagues/Ex-Students) who know their potentials. we then interview certain parameters to see if he has 'IT' in him

  • As a Student

    Students are expected to stay with the trainer. We encourage them to farm as part of the daily routine to learn Engineering process. he will be involved in Open Source projects relevant to his skillsets.

  • Pro-gramming

    students are trained on the latest market trends & technologies by industry experts. There is no predefined training duration period. It goes until he or the trainer feels 'COMPLETE'. While joining, our students may not be the same. But they should be when they 'COMP(L)ETE'

  • Placement

    We reach out to companies that need relevant skill sets. As a principle, we never recommend people we just refer to. When the company and student find themselves matching. Our Student becomes ALUMNI :)
